Platform showcasing a collective’s soundscape collection « Talk with urban landscape ».

Design ✿ Simple, 3 scrolling columns with the name of the participating artists, descriptions and a p5 sound reactive artwork sound-responsive.

Usability ✿ The user can collapse the 2 first columns to allow space for the whole sound-generative artwork to be full screen.

Content ✿ Is the main component of the website. The p5 sound-reactive artwork also is an artistic component, if not artwork in itself.

Creativity ✿ Brutalist aesthetic, which also works conceptually with the soundscape art direction (very rudimentary). Simple aesthetic allows space for the showcased art itself.
