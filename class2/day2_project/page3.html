↩ REVIEW EXERCICE

Artist portfolio of Julijonas Urbonas.

Design ✿ Simple, 3 scrolling columns of « about », « projects » with each their rolling menu, which hyperlinks to different pages; and the portfolio with project documentation such as pictures and texts.

Usability ✿ The user simply scrolls the 3 column, or click on the rolling menu at the top to see more content.

Content ✿ The portfolio section gives a personal and intimate access to the artist work by having a « journal » tone to the documentation. There is a sense of aesthetic cohesiveness that comes from the artist’s artwork.

Creativity ✿ Very neat and the design is coherent. It allows space and attention on the art pieces, but also complements or adds a layer to the artist’s art direction. Even if the artist’s work is extensive, it does not feel heavy to navigate the portfolio.
