Website of the restaurant brand project Málà Project.

Design ✿ Top menu with « menu », « about », « inquire », « order », « catering » and « reserve ». There is also sound on each page that completes the project's universe.

Usability ✿ simple, each menu option leads to a new page designed under the aesthetic of the project. Then each has a full and long div scroll with the infos.

Content ✿ Menu, about, ordering, reserve + food and drink photos and videos + overlay subtitle of the soundscape.

Creativity ✿ 90’s nostalgia aesthetic, merge with Northern chinese aesthetic and references. As it is a branding project, the website itself gives a whole vibe - the brand and can allow the user to almost get a taste of the food (that we haven’t even tasted yet)
